The site is an online store. Explore a wide range of products with smooth shipping and delivery.

Technologies:

  • Next.js 15

  • Typescript

Features:

  • User Authentication: Implemented secure user authentication and authorization to protect user data and enhance security.
  • CRUD Functionality: Developed robust CRUD functionality for managing shopping cart items, allowing users to seamlessly create, update, and delete items with ease.
  • Product Filters: Implemented advanced product filtering options.
  • Language Toggle & Theme Toggle: Provided intuitive options for users to switch between Arabic and English, and choose between light and dark modes, enhancing personalization.
  • Responsive Design: Ensured seamless user experience across all devices.
  • SEO Friendly: Enhanced for better search engine visibility.
